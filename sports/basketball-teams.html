Basketball Teams

National Basketball Teams
Men

The Malta men's national basketball team represents Malta in international basketball. They are controlled by the Malta Basketball Association. Malta has been a FIBA member since 1967, and competes at tournaments such as the European Championship for Small Countries and the Games of the Small States of Europe.

Women

The Malta women's national basketball team represents Malta in international women's basketball competitions.

Men U18

The Malta men's national under-18 basketball team is a national basketball team of Malta, administered by the Malta Basketball Association. It represents the country in international men's under-18 basketball competitions.

Women U18

The Malta women's national under-18 basketball team is a national basketball team of Malta, administered by the Malta Basketball Association. It represents the country in women's international under-18 basketball competitions.

Men U16

The Malta men's national under-16 basketball team is a national basketball team of Malta, administered by the Malta Basketball Association. It represents the country in international men's under-16 basketball competitions.

Women U16
